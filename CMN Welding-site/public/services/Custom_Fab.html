Custom Fabrication

At CMN Welding and Hydraulics, we bring your ideas to life with precision-built, high-strength metal fabrication. Whether you need a one-off custom piece, structural components, or fully engineered assemblies, we deliver craftsmanship that stands up to real-world use.

What We Offer

  • Custom brackets, mounts, and structural components
  • Handrails, frames, and supports
  • Custom-built equipment and attachments
  • Sheet metal cutting, forming, and shaping
  • Precision TIG, MIG, and Stick welding
  • Repairs, reinforcement, and modifications

Why Choose CMN?

Every project is built with durability, accuracy, and attention to detail. As a veteran-owned business, we take pride in delivering work that reflects discipline, reliability, and craftsmanship. We work closely with you to understand your needs and ensure the final product meets your expectations.
