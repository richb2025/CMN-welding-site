About Us

CMN Welding and Hydraulics LLC is a veteran-owned welding and fabrication shop based in Long Prairie, MN. We specialize in custom metal fabrication, structural welding, equipment repairs, and hydraulic services for individuals, farms, and businesses across the region.

Our Story

Founded on a commitment to quality and craftsmanship, CMN Welding brings military discipline and precision to every project. We take pride in building and repairing things that last from custom trailers and gates to heavy equipment repairs and hydraulic cylinder rebuilds.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Veteran owned and operated discipline and reliability in every job
  • Local to Long Prairie, MN serving the surrounding communities
  • Hands-on craftsmanship with attention to detail
  • Honest pricing and clear communication
  • Mobile welding available for select projects
